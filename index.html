Who are we?


SIIA, stands for Skilled immigrants in America. We are documented, legal, skilled immigrants currently working and residing in US. We work legally on non-immigrant visa status, pay all taxes, follow rules and significant investor in American economy.

What we want?


Our primary objective is to create awareness about employment based green and backlogs and drealted issues affecting thousands of legal immigrants primary from the countries like Indian and China. We want US Congress to act in favor of us law abiding Skilled Immigrants by resolving existing unfair and discriminatory green card allotment process which is based on race and country of origin and on merit.
